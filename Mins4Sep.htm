·          Risk Methodology

o   It was agreed by all team members that the Open FAIR risk methodology would be used for the case study

o   The justification was that Open FAIR is a framework which can be applied to a small organisation such as pampered pets, which has 4 employees.  NIST is another framework but is focused on the SDLC and software development. Pampered pets is too small an organisation to propose any internal software builds.

o   ISO is another framework but this is geared towards large organizations and the time to work through this approach couldn’t be justified for Pampered pets.

o   Octave can be used to supplement open FAIR, but open FAIR is a free product, whereas Octave is not

 

o   It was agreed by the group that the approach to the risk analysis should be qualitative and not quantitative, because the data for a quantitative assessment does not exist

 

o   It was agreed that the justification for the risk methodology, and the approach taken to threat modelling would be written up by Jane and circulated to the group for review (Action: Jane)
