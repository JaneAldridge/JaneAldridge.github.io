Executive Summary

The team was tasked with enumerating the potential risks to the quality and supply chain for a Pet Supply business. The project involved the selection of a quantitative risk modelling approach with justification. An explanation of the calculations carried out, and the results of the quantitative models used. In addition the project also included a summary of the results and recommendations around the risk of loss of quality and the risk of supply chain issues. Finally the report included a proposal for a DR strategy and also how to avoid vendor lock-in.
